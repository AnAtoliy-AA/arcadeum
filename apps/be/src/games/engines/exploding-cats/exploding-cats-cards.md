# Exploding Kittens Card Reference Guide

This document outlines the mechanics and descriptions for all cards in the game.

### Core Explosion Mechanics

- **Exploding Kitten**: If drawn, the player must show the card immediately. If they cannot defuse it, they are eliminated from the game.
- **Defuse**: Allows a player to put a drawn Exploding Kitten back into the deck at any location.

### Turn Management

- **Attack (2x)**: Ends the player's turn without drawing and forces the next player to take two turns.
- **Skip**: Ends the current player's turn without drawing a card.

### Future Sight

- **See the Future (3x)**: Privately view the top three cards of the draw pile.
- **Shuffle**: Shuffles the draw pile.

### Player Interaction

- **Favor**: Forces one player to give you a card of their choice from their hand.
- **Nope**: Stops any action from another player and can be played at any time.

### Cat Cards (for combos)

- **Tacocat**: Powerless alone, but pair steals random card.
- **Hairy Potato Cat**: Powerless alone, but pair steals random card.
- **Rainbow Ralphing Cat**: Powerless alone, but pair steals random card.
- **Cattermelon**: Powerless alone, but pair steals random card.
- **Bearded Cat**: Powerless alone, but pair steals random card.

### Expansion 1: Attack Pack

- **Targeted Attack (2x)**: Ends the turn without drawing and forces a chosen player to take two turns.
- **Personal Attack (3x)**: The player who plays this card must take three turns in a row.
- **Attack of the Dead**: The player does not draw; the next player must take three turns for every dead player in the game.
- **Super Skip**: Ends all remaining turns for the current player without drawing.
- **Reverse**: Reverses the order of play and ends the turn without drawing.

### Expansion 2: Future Pack

- **See the Future (5x)**: Privately view the top five cards of the draw pile.
- **Alter the Future (3x / 5x)**: Privately view and rearrange the top three or five cards of the draw pile.
- **Reveal the Future (3x)**: Show all players the top three cards of the deck.
- **Share the Future (3x)**: View and rearrange the top three cards, then show them to the next player.
- **Draw From the Bottom**: Ends the turn by drawing the bottom card of the deck.
- **Swap Top and Bottom**: Swaps the top card and bottom card of the draw pile.
- **Bury**: Ends the turn by secretly putting the next card drawn back into the deck.

### Expansion 3: Theft Pack

- **Feral Cat**: Can be used as any cat card.
- **Mark**: Pick a player and a random card from their hand; they must keep that card facing outward until played or stolen.
- **I'll Take That**: Place in front of another player to steal the next card they draw from the deck.
- **Tower of Power**: Fill with stash cards; anyone who steals from you must draw from the tower instead of your hand.

### Expansion 4: Chaos Pack

- **Imploding Kitten**: When drawn face down, it is placed back in the deck face up without requiring a Defuse. If drawn face up, the player explodes immediately and cannot use a Defuse.
- **Streaking Kitten**: Allows a player to secretly hold one Exploding Kitten in their hand without blowing up.
- **Catomic Bomb**: Removes all Exploding Kittens from the deck, shuffles the deck, and places the Kittens back on top; ends the player's turn.
- **Potluck**: Every player, starting with the person who played the card, puts one card on top of the draw pile.
- **Curse of the Cat Butt**: Makes a player "blind," forcing them to shuffle their hand and play cards face down until they draw.

### Expansion 5: Deity Pack

- **Zombie Kitten**: Instead of exploding, the player secretly replaces the Exploding Kitten into the draw pile and brings a dead player back to life.
- **Devilcat**: Causes a player to explode if obtained during an Armageddon unless they discard a Defuse.
- **Armageddon**: Triggers an event and ends the turn; only playable if Godcat is on the playmat.
- **Godcat**: Can be played as any card in the game except a Nope.
